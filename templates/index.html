About Us

OUR CENTERS: Vellore || Indore || Varanasi || Pune

WHAT MAKES US DIFFERENT?
We provide great services and beauty suggestions using Machine Learning Technology!

On Fleek Salon & Spa offers all of the creative, healthy hair care services and beauty treatment products you seek from a salon inside of a tranquil, professional atmosphere. Located in 4 cities in India- Vellore, Indore, Varanasi, and Pune our technicians and licensed stylists offer a wide range of services tailored for women. Total client satisfaction is our top priority, and we work to ensure that daily by providing friendly, timely service, as well as one-on-one consultations that allow clients to share the unique vision for their hair care and beauty needs. Call or visit our salon today for appointment scheduling and detailed information about our services.

The best salon in this part of India with an ever increasing exceptional service delivery,committed to bringing out your Extraordinary beauty. On Fleek Salon & Spa is a well finished 5-star salon and spa, laid out in its own world of a fabulous and luxurious environment, a world of exceptional taste and style dedicated to the classy and sophisticated make over for very taste-conscious and important people.

Our beauty parlour is designed with contemporary elegance, and combines the most respected hair, nails and facial beauty treatment methods known to mankind with the most unique and unforgettable hospitality and stylish finish.
